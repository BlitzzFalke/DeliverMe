deliver me

slow, unreliable mail

Download Download View deliver me on GitHub

the deliver me project is a network of drop points that lets you send and deliver messages in a different and exciting way

your mission, should you choose to accept it:

help deliver a letter by bringing it to its recipient, dropping it off at another deliver me drop point closer to its destination, or handing it to another person that will get the letter closer to its destination.

send a message of your own:

if you have your own paper, you can write a message and use one of our envelopes to send it. write someone’s name and address and put it in the mailbox for someone to deliver! if you don’t have your own paper, it’s probably okay to write on the inside of the envelope.

deliver me is a work in progress. comments and suggestions are welcome
